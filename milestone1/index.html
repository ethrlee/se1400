Website Summary

What content will this site contain?

This website will contain information about my personal collection of guitars, amps, and effects. It will also have the settings for each piece of gear given a specific song or artist i want to emulate.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

I will be using my own personal collection of gear to take pictures and write the text for the site. I will also be using youtube videos and other guitar related websites.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users should care about the focus of the site because it will give them a good idea of what gear they need to get a specific sound. It will also give them a good idea of what gear is out there and what it can do.

Why should a site be built for this?

This site should be built mainly for my own record of how to achieve the sounds that i like. It will also be a good resource for other guitar players who are looking to achieve similar sounds.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to explore the site and find new gear that they might be interested in. I would also like them to try out the settings that i have provided and see if they can achieve the same sound.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of the site is to provide a resource for guitar players who are looking to achieve specific sounds. It will also be a good record for myself of how to achieve those sounds.
